Garlic Shrimp with Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Knorr Arroz Blanco Sazonado
  • 1 kg raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 dozen flour tortillas
  • ½ cup chopped parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil and melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Stir in the chopped parsley and cook for one more minute.
  5. Cook a Knorr Arroz Blanco Sazonado pouch per the package instructions. Let it rest for 5 minutes after cooking.
  6. Serve the garlic shrimp over a bed of white garlic-flavored Mexican rice.
  7. Serve with lime wedges on the side or squeeze lime juice over the dish. Enjoy!
